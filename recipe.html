Recipe for Egg Omlette

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Sliced Onion
  • Sliced Tomato
  • Chilli Powder
  • Salt

Procedure

  1. Take a bowl and crack the eggs into them
  2. Add the sliced tomatoes and onions to the bowl
  3. Add required amount of salt and chilli powder
  4. Mix them well and heat the pan
  5. Spread the mixture and the pan and allow it to cook
  6. Take the omlette into a plate and serve with sauce
